  • Spreading Awareness

    Several factors have contributed to burgeoning issues of environmental degradation and climate change that we face today. The unruly disposal of e-waste contributes significantly to the same and has resulted in very poor air quality. Our aim is to spread awareness on clean, and more sustainable methods of e-waste disposal to protect the environment.

How it Works

A Simple Process

On scheduling your e-waste pickup through our call centre, we will ensure a timely pick up from your site to our R2 certified recycling plant to ensure a complete end to life solution of your e-waste disposal. The reverse logistics is managed by our own fleet of vehicles and channel partners from nation-wide locations.
